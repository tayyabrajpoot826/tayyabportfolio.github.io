IN THE BEGINNING

After years of experience in the welding and fabrication, moulded with a keen eye for quality it was time to start creating a name for ourselves. Originally Binks Auto Fabrications, the long-winded name just had to vanish, and so focusing more and more onto track focused vehicles each year - the name BAF Motorsports was brought to the front line.

WHAT WE DO

Based in Yorkshire we have become well known for creating some of the best custom exhaust systems in our local area. But that does not mean we are limited to just that one line of work.

The K-Brace is our proudest creation, with over 100 bespoke vehicle designs and shipping worldwide, vehicle handling and stability will never be the same.

Bespoke performance parts such as intakes, spoilers and boost pipes are well within our calibre, with 100’s of projects under our belt we are capable of all your desires.

NEED A REPAIR?

From alloy wheels to manifolds we can repair just about anything whether it be a crack or reconstruction - we are here to help.

Our aim is to improve not only ourselves but to enhance the products that are available to you. We will continue to grow the K-Brace collection until the end of time, while expanding our product range in other fields of work.
